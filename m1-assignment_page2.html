Marvel Studios

Marvel Studios, LLC (originally known as Marvel Films from 1993 to 1996) is an American film and television production company that is a subsidiary of Walt Disney Studios, a division of The Walt Disney Company. Marvel Studios is known for the production of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) franchise, based on characters that appear in Marvel Comics publications.

History

Marvel Studios was founded in 1993 as Marvel Films. In the early 2000s, the studio began producing its own films, starting with Iron Man (2008), which initiated the MCU. The MCU has become the highest-grossing film franchise of all time.
